01 — About

Who I am

I'm a fresh graduate of BS Mathematics (Applied Statistics) from Bulacan State University, where I graduated Magna Cum Laude in 2025. My work sits at the intersection of data-driven thinking and human-centered design.

As a freelance Instructional Designer at TAGLearning, I developed 13 e-learning courses for special education professionals — translating dense legal and procedural content into structured, learner-centered experiences using Articulate Rise 360 and Thinkific.

I'm passionate about how well-designed learning experiences can genuinely change what people know and can do. I'm eager to bring that passion to a collaborative ID team where data and design work together.
